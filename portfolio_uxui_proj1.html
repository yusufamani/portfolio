Aquarium Landing Page


UX UI



The Hero Section is the starting point of a User's experience when visiting a website. This first encounter is crucial when influencing how users feel, establishing recognizability within a brand, and increasing conversion. I designed the Hero Section of a hypothetical Aquarium with the intention of incorporating my original flat illustrations into the User Interface. There are many benefits to blending illustrations into a UI, such as simplifying concepts and establishing a clear message for users to understand. I love utilizing flat design concepts because they add a level of minimalizm, simplicity, cohesion, and functionality to the design of a UI.
